About

HackConf is one of the largest tech and motivational conferences in Bulgaria. We at HackBulgaria are organizing it for a third consecutive year, on 30th of September and 1st of October 2017. The event will take place in Hall "John Atanasov" at Sofia Tech Park. The main purpose of the conference is to support the progress of developers, software engineers and IT specialists - and help them to become better in their work. Junior and Senior developers from all around the country are welcomed to gain new knowledge and meet professionals of the IT sector. You can find more about the organizers here: hackbulgaria.com
